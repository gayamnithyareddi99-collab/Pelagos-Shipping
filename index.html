How We Work

From Booking to Final-Mile Delivery

Every Pelagos shipment follows a proven four-stage journey from quote and booking through customs clearance, ocean or air transit, and seamless final-mile delivery with real-time tracking at every step.

STEP 01

Quote & Book

Submit your cargo details and receive a competitive freight quote within hours. Our logistics team selects the optimal route, carrier, and mode of transport tailored to your timeline and budget.

STEP 02

Customs & Documentation

Our certified customs brokers handle all export/import documentation, HS code classification, and regulatory compliance ensuring your cargo clears borders without delays or fines.

STEP 03

Ocean, Air & Land Transit

Cargo is loaded and tracked across our global carrier network FCL/LCL ocean containers, air freight pallets, or multimodal land corridors with live GPS tracking and automated status alerts.

STEP 04

Delivery & Confirmation

Final-mile delivery is coordinated from port to warehouse or door, with digital proof-of-delivery, cargo insurance settlement if needed, and post-shipment analytics for supply chain optimisation.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is the difference between FCL and LCL ocean freight?

+
FCL (Full Container Load) means your cargo exclusively fills a 20ft or 40ft container ideal for large shipments. LCL (Less than Container Load) means your cargo shares container space with other shippers' goods and you pay only for the cubic metres or kilograms you use. Pelagos offers both options across all major trade lanes, with our team helping you choose the most cost-effective solution based on your cargo dimensions and timeline.

How does Pelagos handle customs clearance?

+
Our in-house licensed customs brokers manage the complete clearance process HS code classification, import/export declarations, duty and VAT calculation, AEO compliance, and sanctions screening. We maintain 99.4% first-pass clearance success by ensuring all documentation is correct before cargo arrives at the border, eliminating costly delays and penalties.

Can Pelagos handle dangerous goods or temperature-sensitive cargo?

+
Yes. Pelagos is IATA-certified for dangerous goods air freight and works with specialist ocean carriers for IMO-classified sea freight. For temperature-controlled cargo, we offer refrigerated containers (reefers) for ocean freight and temperature-controlled air cargo ULDs. All handling is compliant with ADR, IMDG, and ICAO regulations.

What is your carbon reduction commitment?

+
Pelagos is committed to supporting the IMO's target of net-zero shipping emissions by 2050. We offer carbon footprint reporting for all shipments, partner with carriers investing in LNG and methanol propulsion, and provide clients with green shipping options. Our ESG reporting suite enables enterprise clients to include shipping emissions in their sustainability disclosures.
